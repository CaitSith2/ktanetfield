Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Text Field

On the Subject of Text Field

It's trivial. Just 6 letters out there! Wait, what did you say? Each letter has 5 rules to check? Nevermind then.

  • This modules contains 3*4 letter field, all letters in the field will be the same. (Available letters: A-F)
  • Based on the letter, see Section A and follows the ruleset for that letter from top to bottom. You will get table name from the first rule that applies.
  • Go to Section B and find the corresponding table based on the last step.
  • Select all letters in the field that matches the table. The module will disarm when all matched letters have been selected.

See Appendix A for indicator identification reference.
See Appendix B for battery identification reference.
See Appendix C for port identification reference.

Section A: Letters rulesets

Letter A
- Has CLR indicator: 1459
- Has more than 2 batteries: BBFF
- Has 1 battery: 7F67
- Has FRK indicator: DC52
- Otherwise: A0C1

Letter C
- Has DVI-D port: AA12
- Has 2 batteries: FB01
- No vowels in serial number: DC52
- Has CAR indicator: 1459
- Otherwise: 7F67

Letter E
- Has less than 3 batteries: 7F67
- No stereo RCA port: AA12
- Has BOB indicator: A0C1
- Has RJ-45 port: BBFF
- Otherwise: AC62

 Letter B
- No battery: 965A
- No letters in serial number: 1459
- No Serial port: DC52
- Has TRN indicator: A0C1
- Otherwise: 7F67

Letter D
- Has Parallel port: FB01
- Has less than 2 batteries: AA12
- Has SIG indicator: BBFF
- No indicator: 965A
- Otherwise: 1459

Letter F
- No Serial port: DC52
- Has vowels in serial number: A0C1
- Has IND indicator: 1459
- Has numbers in serial number: FB01
- Otherwise: AA12
